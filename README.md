                                    **Customer Churn Analysis**

**Project Overview**

This project analyzes customer churn behaviour to identify the reasons behind it and also key factors that are influencing retention. The goal is to help business reduce churn count by understanding customer needs and patterns.

**Objectives**

* Analyze churn rate
* Understand factors affectibg churn
* Identify patterns
* Providing business recommendations

**Tools used**

* SQL
* Tableau
* Excel

**Key Insights**

* Customers with short tenure (0 - 6 months) have highest churn rate
* Month to month increase churn probability
* Pricing also playing a huge role in churning

**Conclusion**

Businesses can reduce churn by offfering long term contracts, discounts and pricing strategies and identifying and understanding needs of the customers.
